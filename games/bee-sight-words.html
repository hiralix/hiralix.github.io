Bee Sight Words

Fly & find the matching word!

How to play: Read the word at the top. Move your bee with the arrow keys or the touch buttons and land on the flower that shows the same word. Collect the right flower to earn points!

🏆 High Score: 0

❓ How To Play

1. A sight word appears at the top of the screen.

2. Look at the flowers floating in the garden — each one has a word on it.

3. Use the arrow keys (or the on-screen buttons) to fly your bee to the flower that matches the top word.

4. Correct flower = sparkles, a happy sound, and +10 points! ✨

5. Wrong flower = the flower shakes and you lose one heart ❤️.

6. You have 3 hearts and 60 seconds. Score enough points before time runs out to win!
